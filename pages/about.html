Making charitable data more accessible

Grantmakers.io is a community project. Its simple goal is to demonstrate how open source technologies can be leveraged to minimize the cost of hosting and sharing philanthropic data.

Features

Live Search
Simple and fast

Thanks to our friends at Algolia, you get immediate results from your queries. Search by EIN, foundation name, and location among many others.

Summary Data
Clear and concise

No need to tediously scan Form 990-PF images. All the basic information you need to quickly understand a grantmaker's giving profile.

Expanded Profiles
Relevant and detailed

Detailed information on staff, board members, recent grants, giving levels and more.
